# El Tesoro de Patitas (Patitas' Treasure)

A delicious and healthy low-carb poke bowl, specially crafted for Patitas, featuring perfectly cooked salmon and fresh vegetables.

## Ingredients

*   170g cooked salmon
*   Mixed greens (purple lettuce, escarole, spinach), about 60% of the plate, finely cut
*   30g quinoa
*   30g corn
*   50g cucumber, deseeded and finely diced
*   Half an avocado, sliced or diced

### For the Salmon Seasoning

*   1 tsp garlic powder
*   1/2 tsp ginger powder
*   1/4 tsp paprika (optional, for color)
*   1 tsp sesame oil
*   Salt to taste

### For the Dressing

*   2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
*   1 tsp sesame oil
*   1 tsp rice vinegar or lime juice (optional, for brightness)

## Instructions

1.  **Prepare the Salmon:** Season the cooked salmon with garlic powder, ginger powder, paprika (if using), sesame oil, and salt. Gently flake the salmon into bite-sized pieces.
2.  **Prepare the Vegetables:** In a large bowl, combine the finely cut mixed greens, quinoa, corn, deseeded cucumber, and avocado.
3.  **Make the Dressing:** In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar or lime juice (if using).
4.  **Assemble the Bowl:** Arrange the mixed greens as the base of the bowl. Artfully place the quinoa, corn, cucumber, and avocado on top of the greens.
5.  **Add the Salmon:** Gently place the seasoned salmon pieces on top of the vegetables.
6.  **Dress and Serve:** Drizzle the dressing generously over the entire bowl. Serve immediately and enjoy "El Tesoro de Patitas"!

## Optional Low-Carb Additions

*   **Edamame:** For extra protein and texture.
*   **Wakame Salad:** For a savory, umami boost.
*   **Crispy Onions:** For a delightful crunch.
