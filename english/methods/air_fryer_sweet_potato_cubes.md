# Air Fryer Sweet Potato Cubes

This method is great for a quick side dish or addition to bowls.

## Ingredients

*   1 lb sweet potatoes
*   1 tbsp olive oil
*   1 tsp garlic powder
*   1/2 tsp paprika
*   Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1.  **Preparation:** Peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into 1/2 to 1-inch cubes. Aim for uniform size to ensure even cooking.
2.  **Seasoning:** In a bowl, toss the sweet potato pieces with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
3.  **Air Frying:** Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the seasoned sweet potatoes in a single layer in the air fryer basket, cooking in batches if necessary to avoid overcrowding.
4.  **Cooking:** Air fry for 10-12 minutes, shaking the basket or stirring halfway through to ensure even cooking and crispiness.
